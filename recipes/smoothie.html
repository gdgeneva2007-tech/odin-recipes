Smoothie

Descrption

A fruit smoothie is a quick, healthy, blended beverage typically made from fresh or frozen fruits, a liquid base like milk or juice, and sometimes yogurt or other add-ins for thickness and protein. The consistency can range from a drinkable juice to a thick, sherbet-like mixture depending on the ingredients and amount of ice used.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mixed fruit (e.g., strawberries, bananas, mango, berries), fresh or frozen
  • ¾ cup milk (dairy or non-dairy like almond or oat milk) or juice
  • ½ cup plain Greek yogurt (optional, for creaminess and protein)
  • Ice cubes (optional, if using fresh fruit)

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients: Place all ingredients into a blender. Put softer items and liquids near the blade first for smoother blending
  2. Blend: Blend on high speed until the mixture is completely smooth and reaches your desired consistency.
  3. Serve: Pour into glasses and serve immediately.
