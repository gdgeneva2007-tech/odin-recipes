Tacos

Descrption

Ground beef tacos are a simple, family-friendly meal, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner. They consist of seasoned ground beef served in a warm tortilla or crispy shell with various fresh toppings like shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning (or homemade blend of chili powder, cumin, etc.)
  • Â¾ cup water or tomato sauce
  • 8-12 taco shells or tortillas
  • Toppings: Shredded cheese, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sour cream, guacamole

Steps

  1. Cook the beef: Brown the ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it apart with a spoon. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Season: Stir in the taco seasoning and water (or tomato sauce). Bring the mixture to a simmer.
  3. Simmer: Reduce the heat to low and simmer for about 5 minutes, or until the liquid has mostly evaporated and the mixture has thickened.
  4. Warm shells: While the meat simmers, warm your taco shells or tortillas in the oven or microwave according to package directions.
  5. Assemble and serve: Spoon the seasoned meat into the shells and add your favorite toppings.
